concise

adjective
/kənˈsaɪs/

Meaning

Expressing much in few words; short and clear.

Examples

  • Keep your email concise.
  • The guide is concise and useful.
  • Please be concise during the meeting.

How to use it

Often used with “be” (be concise) or before a noun (a concise summary).
